About Us

Our commitment is to restore individuals to a life of fullness through community and compassionate care.

Our Mission & Vision

Byuzuye Pivot Organization (BPO) was founded on the belief that mental health is a fundamental human right. Our vision is to be a premier hub for comprehensive care for individuals with mental illness and their families in Rwanda and beyond.

Our mission is To provide holistic care to people with mental health challenges in Rwanda and beyond through psychiatric services, therapeutic support, rehabilitation and reintegration, community awareness programs and family support alongside proclaiming the gospel of Jesus Christ.

Meet Our Leadership

Our team is composed of passionate and dedicated professionals committed to making a difference.

ISSA NTAMBARA

Founder & Executive Director

Issa founded BPO with a vision to create a world where mental health is understood and everyone has the support to live a full life. His leadership is driven by compassion and a deep commitment to our community.

Emmanuel Habimana

Director of Programs

Emmanuel designs and oversees our support programs, ensuring they are effective, culturally sensitive, and centered on the needs of our participants. He has over 15 years of experience in community health.

Aline Keza

Lead Counselor

Aline provides guidance and support to our counseling team. Her empathetic approach and clinical expertise are invaluable in helping individuals navigate their healing journeys.

David Mugisha

Community Outreach Coordinator

David is the bridge between BPO and the communities we serve. He works tirelessly to raise awareness, build partnerships, and ensure our services are accessible to those who need them most.
