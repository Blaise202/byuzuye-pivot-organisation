About Us

Our commitment is to restore individuals to a life of fullness through community and compassionate care.

Our Mission & Vision

Byuzuye Pivot Organization (BPO) was founded on the belief that mental health is a fundamental human right. Our vision is to be a premier hub for comprehensive care for individuals with mental illness and their families in Rwanda and beyond.

Our mission is To provide holistic care to people with mental health challenges in Rwanda and beyond through psychiatric services, therapeutic support, rehabilitation and reintegration, community awareness programs and family support alongside proclaiming the gospel of Jesus Christ.
